The cafeteria

The university cantine at P35 is the heart of campus, a place where students and employees meet both for eating and studying. It offers both hot and cold food. The buffet offers several warm dishes, soups, salads and much more. For the buffet you pay by weight. The many seatng places across the open landscape with large windows gives it a vibrant feel
